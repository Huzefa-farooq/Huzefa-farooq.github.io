Single Active Bridge vs Dual Active Bridge Analysis

Modeled and compared isolated DC–DC converter topologies to evaluate efficiency, current stress, and soft-switching performance.

Battery Fuel Gauge Modeling in MATLAB

Implemented a full software-based fuel gauge to estimate state of charge, internal resistance, and remaining runtime.

Built a MATLAB model using voltage, current, and time data to estimate battery states that are not directly measurable. The solution integrated numerical current integration, pseudo-OCV construction, SOC lookup generation, resistance estimation, Coulomb counting, and shutdown-time prediction.

✔️ Estimated capacity, SOC, internal resistance, and time to shutdown
✔️ Combined data processing, interpolation, and algorithmic estimation
✔️ Demonstrated practical battery management system concepts through software modeling
